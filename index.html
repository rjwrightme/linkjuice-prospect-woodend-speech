Sound Familiar?

Every parent wants the best start for their child

If you have noticed any of these signs, early intervention with a specialist paediatric speech pathologist can make a real difference.

Hard to Understand

Other people struggle to understand what your child is saying, even though you know what they mean.

Late to Talk

Your child is not using as many words or sentences as other kids their age, and you are worried they are falling behind.

Reading Struggles

School is getting harder. Your child avoids reading, mixes up letters, or cannot sound out new words.

Long Wait Times

Public services have months-long waitlists, and travelling to Melbourne for specialist care is not realistic for your family.

What We Help With

Specialist therapy, right here in Woodend

Our team of four experienced speech pathologists covers the full range of paediatric communication needs, from first words to high school.

Speech Clarity

Articulation therapy and Childhood Apraxia of Speech (CAS) treatment to help your child speak clearly and be understood by everyone, not just family.

Language Development

Targeted support for vocabulary, grammar, sentence structure, and comprehension. We use Hanen programs and evidence-based strategies that parents can use at home.

Literacy & Phonological Awareness

Early literacy skills that set your child up for reading and spelling success. ADA Dyslexia Accredited therapy using structured, evidence-based approaches.

Social Skills & Pragmatic Language

Programs like Talkabout and Social Thinking help children build friendships, read social cues, and navigate conversations with confidence.

AAC & Alternative Communication

PECS, LAMP, PODD, and Key Word Signing for children who need support beyond spoken words. We find the right system for your child and teach the whole family how to use it.

Coordinated Care

We work directly with your child's teachers, OTs, psychologists, GPs, and paediatricians to make sure everyone is pulling in the same direction.
